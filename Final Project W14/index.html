Welcome to the Coalition of Cabbage Connoisseurs Website

Cabbages have been cultivated for tousands of years all across the world. To this day we are unsure of when the first variety of cabbage was domesticated and cultivated, but it is estimated to be at least 2000 years ago.
Over the millenia cabbage has been used for a variety of purposes other than nutrition. It has been used to treat ailments such as headaches, gout, and can even counter the effects of various poisons.
There are some cases where tougher breeds of cabbage have been used in construction. Today in the 21st Century China is the largest producer of cabbage, and is transported to over 100 different countries around the world.
